What is God Hunter?

God-Hunter is a tabletop role-playing game for a game-master and two to five players. It depicts a group of roving, oathbound warriors defending communities by banishing monsters and tearing down tyrants, supported by a caravan of friends and followers. Empires churn. Otherworldly forces meddle. Hunters die, but their souls carry on.
